About Us

Hello! We are Izzi and Charlie, two youngsters with a dream to have our own company. At the moment we are limited to small DIY jobs, but strive to improve our variety of skills in the next few years to become a business that can offer carpentry too, and possibly electrical and plumbing skills. We hope to be a fully functional company in the first few weeks of the new year, with our prices being low and affordable for people who need little handy jobs done!
